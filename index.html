Why PawCare?

PawCare is an initiative by CollegeTips.in to transform Indian cities into pet-friendly spaces. We believe every street animal deserves care, compassion, and a chance at a better life.

Through community engagement, we're building a network of volunteers who feed, rescue, and advocate for our furry friends across the country.
